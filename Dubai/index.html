NIRAV SHAH WINS DUBAI FOREX TRADING CHALLENGE

Trading millionaire and founder of The Platinum Group, Nirav Shah, recently showcased his renowned trading system and won 1st prize at the ninety-day Dubai Forex Trading challenge. The challenge saw fierce competition and was widely covered by media, being telecasted to an audience of FX trading professionals seeking to learn the secrets of his success.

Nirav comes from a long line of successful traders and bankers, with a goal to revolutionise the FX trading space. After a string of achievements and turning £50,000 to £650,000 in just 6 years, Nirav built a team of other professional and institutional traders with over 35 years of combined experience to launch Platinum Trading Academy back in 2009.

The trading academy offers insight into the systems used by traders like Nirav who consistently make substantial profits in the FX space. The academy program leverages innovative strategies and proven knowledge with a goal of placing at least twenty individuals on the path to becoming a millionaire this year.
